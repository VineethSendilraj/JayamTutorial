Our Teaching Approach


  • Teach the concepts with examples
  • Apply those concepts in the real world using some examples
  • Based on the concept taught students will solve some questions during the class to test their understanding
  • End of each lesson, homework will be given
    • Questions will largely be based on various school examination papers
  • Upon completing each topic
    • Topical test will be conduct and feedback will be given to the parents
  • Marked homework’s and test papers will share via online
  • After completing the syllabus
    • Past year school examination paper will be provided for practice
